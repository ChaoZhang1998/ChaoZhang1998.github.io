Brain Storm

Fourth panel discussion, 11 October 2020

On the evening of October 11, 2020, the group gathered in dorm 806 for another lively discussion on the topics chosen for this week's mini-assignment and the final big assignment.

The following are excerpts of incomplete thoughts.
1. Blind photo recognition, automatically generate diary-style text, perceive and record life, while linking the drawing board to generate touchable images.
2. Wearable devices such as bracelets recognize movements, through different movements and postures, make sounds of different musical instruments.
3. Children collect nature sounds and record them in the form of cards.
4. Make everything around you become a musical instrument, designed for people with sensory disorders.
5. Set up a program for people who have no foundation in music theory to compose music through postures and movements.
6. Seniors exercise their memory by recreating old photos with a jigsaw puzzle
