Love Separated by Distance, IoT Can Pass.

Sixth panel discussion, 18 November 2020

Some people love each other, some people drive at night to see the ocean, and some people can't do their Arduino homework...

有人相爱，有人夜里开车看海，也有人Arduino作业做不出来...

This assignment requires an object to be made using IoT technology. The Internet of Things (IoT, Internet of things) is an extension of the Internet and an expanded network that combines various information-sensing devices with the Internet to form a giant network that enables the connection of people, machines and things at any time and any place. Interoperability.

这次的作业要求是采用IoT技术做一个物件。物联网（ IoT ，Internet of things ）即“万物相连的互联网”，是互联网基础上的延伸和扩展的网络，将各种信息传感设备与互联网结合起来而形成的一个巨大网络，实现在任何时间、任何地点，人、机、物的互联互通。

The panel discussion started as scheduled at the old location 806.

小组讨论如期在老地点806展开。

The first thing Chao proposed was to create a home temperature and humidity detector that would allow users to get real-time data from their cell phones.

首先超哥提议的是制作一个家庭温湿度检测器，用户可以通过手机实时得到数据。

"But if you're outside, why do you need to know the temperature and humidity of your home remotely?" It seems to be missing the IoT meaning of "remote interactive network".

“但是你如果在外面，为什么要远程知道家里的温湿度的？” 好像少了IoT那种“远程交互网络”的意思。

Then, Ming Yue proposed a "smart eye" that could be placed at the front door of the house, allowing users to remotely monitor the people outside the door, as well as the visitors when no one is home. However, due to the shortage of equipment and parts on hand, the idea was not very feasible.

随后，明月提出可以制作一个安置在家门口的“智能眼睛”，用户通过手机远程监控门外的人员情况，也可以得知家内无人时上门访客情况。但是碍于手头设备和零件的短缺，这个想法可行性不高。

Looking around, and considering the components we had on hand, a new idea popped up: "We could make a gadget for long-distance lovers to enhance their relationship!" "Remote light alert, just as we have the rest of the lights!" "You can borrow YYClub's heart-shaped light, hahahahaha..."

环顾四周，以及结合手头上拥有的元器件考虑，新的想法随即迸发：“我们可以做一个给异地恋情侣的，增进感情的小玩意儿！”“远程亮灯提醒，正好我们有剩余的灯！”“可以借用YYClub的心形灯，哈哈哈哈...”

The design direction of this IoT mini-assignment was then happily decided.

于是这个IoT小作业的设计方向就愉快地决定了。

"What should we name it?"
"Might as well call it, the signal of thoughts."

“给它取个什么名字好呢？”
“不如就叫，思念的信号。”

People who are in love are often poets.

恋爱中的人往往是诗人啊。
