Schrödinger's Final Project

Seventh panel discussion, 29 November 2020

After much discussion, rejection, and re-discussion, the final project design was finalized.

Schrödinger's "Final Project" refers to the inspiration for the final project of the Natural team, which exists in fragments in the minds of the team members. The human mind is subject to forgetting and mutation. If the cognitive upgrade occurs after many discussions of the collision of ideas, the original discussion results will be broken and the direction of the big job will be changed; if there is no discussion, the direction of the big job will remain the same.

薛定谔的“大作业”，是指the Natural小组的最终项目灵感，碎片化地存在于小组成员的脑海里。而人的思绪存在遗忘和变异几率。如果多次讨论思想碰撞发生认知升级，就会打破原有的讨论结果，大作业的方向就会改变；如果不进行讨论，大作业方向就不变。

Hahahahaha, the whole story is just to show that our final project started in early October, and after continuous discussion, data collection, and discussion again, we seem to have entered a bottleneck, and the direction of the project is repeatedly bouncing between new ideas and initial goals.

哈哈哈哈，说了一通只是为了表达我们的最终项目从十月初就开始萌发，经过不断的讨论、搜集资料、再讨论，仿佛进入一个瓶颈，项目方向在新想法与最初目标反复横跳。

最终我们的Final Project初步设计目标如下：

Product Name: Music block sand table

产品名称：音乐积木沙盘

Design Introduction: Creating music in the way of building blocks scenes. This design aims to materialize the characteristics of the basic elements of music, so as to realize the materialization of music form and facilitate children to feel and understand. Secondly, the screen screen displays the image of blocks placed in the sand table in real time, enriching the sensory experience of the game.

设计说明：以积木场景搭建的方式创作音乐。本设计旨在将音乐基本元素特征实体化，从而实现音乐形态的具象化，便于儿童感受与理解。其次，屏幕画面实时显示沙盘中所放置积木形象，丰富游戏感官体验。

It consists of two parts, a sand table with a display screen and a block assembly (Figure 1.1 is temporarily missing).
(1) First of all, building blocks: During the building process, the cognition of the shape, size and color of building blocks modules meets the needs of children's fine movement exercise.
(2) Second, substantiate the basic elements of music: shape, size, arrangement density, color and other physical characteristics are mapped with the rhythm, volume, density of drums and timbre of music, so that children can feel and understand music through multi-dimensional senses.
(3) Third, interactive mode of building blocks scene: arrange the blocks and combine and overlay the music elements.
(4) Besides, screen display: We also set the screen to display more vivid images and pictures of the blocks on the screen in real time, so as to provide children with more immersive game experience.
(5) Finally, music postcard: Children can choose to keep the picture on the screen and print it into a postcard with a QR code, which contains information about the finished music they have created. On the one hand, the child can share the music postcard with a friend, or assemble a portfolio and bind it into a book. On the other hand, children can reproduce and repeat music according to the pictures on the postcards they receive.

它共有两部分组成，一个带有显示屏的沙盘，一份积木组件。
（1）首先，积木搭建：在搭建过程中，对积木模块的形状、大小、色彩等认知符合儿童精细化动作锻炼的需求。
（2）第二，音乐基本元素特征实体化：将形状、大小、排列密度、色彩等实体特征与音乐的节奏、音量、鼓点疏密、音色等建立映射，便于儿童通过多维度感官感受并理解音乐。
（3）第三，积木场景搭建交互方式：通过积木摆放排列，对音乐元素进行组合叠加。
（4）除此以外，屏幕展示：我们还设置了屏幕，将放置的积木实时在屏幕中展示出更生动的形象与画面，提供给孩子更沉浸式的游戏体验。
（5）最后，音乐明信片：儿童可以选择将屏幕中画面保留并打印成带有二维码的明信片，其中二维码包含所创作音乐成品信息。一方面，儿童可以将此音乐明信片分享给小伙伴，或是集成一个作品集，装订成册。另一方面，儿童可以根据收到的明信片上的画面来还原、重奏音乐。
